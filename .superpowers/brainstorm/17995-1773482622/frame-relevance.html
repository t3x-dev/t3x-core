With LLM relevance (toggle ON)

Key difference: Without LLM, "diet: vegetarian" and "shellfish_allergy" fade because they're from turn 2 (old). With LLM, it recognizes these are core to the user's intent — diet shapes every restaurant choice, allergy is safety-critical. LLM re-highlights what the deterministic model would miss.
